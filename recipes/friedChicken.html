How to make fried chicken.

Here is a recipe on how to make jolibee style fried chicken
jolibee is like the better version of kfc because they have a wider variety of cousines and specials. I think in the philipines they have adobo rice which I look forward to trying in the future.
Fried chicken is the perfect food because it can be eaten as a hearty meal or just a small snack. It is very convenient and delicious most of all.

Ingredients:

  • Pepper
  • Buttermilk
  • Egg
  • Cornflour
  • Flour

Steps:

  1. Marinate
  2. Mix
  3. Prepare seasoning/batter
  4. Dip in buttermilk and dry batter
  5. Fry
